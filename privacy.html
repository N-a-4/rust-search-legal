Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

1. Information We Collect

RUST SEARCH may collect and process the following information required for application functionality:

  • Discord User IDs
  • Discord Server IDs
  • Discord Channel IDs
  • Discord Role Information
  • Discord Message Content (for moderation and anti-spam purposes)
  • Discord Presence Information (online, offline, idle, do-not-disturb status)
  • Voice activity statistics
  • Moderation and activity logs
  • Configuration and server settings data

2. How We Use Information

Collected information is used solely to operate and improve the application, including:

  • Automated moderation
  • Anti-spam protection
  • Community management
  • Role synchronization
  • Member statistics
  • Online member statistics
  • Voice activity statistics
  • Application diagnostics and troubleshooting

3. Message Content Processing

Message content is accessed exclusively for moderation, anti-spam protection, rule enforcement, and related community management features. Message content is not sold, shared for advertising purposes, or used for user profiling.

4. Data Sharing

RUST SEARCH does not sell, rent, or distribute user information to third parties, except where required by applicable law.

5. Data Retention

Information is retained only for as long as reasonably necessary to provide application functionality, moderation features, and operational requirements.

6. Data Security

Reasonable technical and organizational measures are implemented to protect collected information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

7. User Rights

Users may request deletion of stored information associated with their Discord account, where technically feasible and legally permitted.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Continued use of the application constitutes acceptance of any updated version.

9. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, please contact the application owner through the official RUST SEARCH Discord community.
